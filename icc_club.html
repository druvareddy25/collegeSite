INNOVATION AND CREATIVITY CLUB

An innovation is something original, novel and important that obtains a foothold in a market or society. It refers to the notion of inventing something which has not existed in the past and which ultimately leads to the advancement, in the society and in the world. Creativity on the other hand is characterized by originality, expressiveness and imagination. It is a skill that every human possesses in one's domain or the other. This skill in the individuals can be further more nourished through various methods. The skill can be creating an idea, a joke, an artistic or literary work, a painting or musical composition, a solution or even an invention. Having taken its birth on 26th August’13 as INNOVATION AND CREATIVITY CLUB at SNIST, it has been putting on tremendous efforts in bringing out the creativity and Novel ideas from the younger generation.

Platforms:

  1. Mayavi is a platform that brings out the talents in designing, video editing, website designing, promos, posters etc...
  2. Aavishkar is a platform that boosts up the new and innovative projects in different fields from the students and guides them in every step in turning their idea into reality.
  3. Laghu Chitra signifies the Short Film Making. It aims at training the students who show great interest in making films
  4. Kala Srushti is an area wherein in Art is given utmost importance. This platform emboldens all the forms of handicrafts including Painting, Craft making, Quilling art, Sketching and many such Ornamental and Creative Arts that add color to any event and place.
  5. This platform aims at bringing out the Talent and Skill from the students who take immense interest in the field of business and entrepreneurship.
  6. Creative Writing is the right platform for the future poets, writers and authors of India. This platform spurs the new thoughts of the younger generation in the form of poems and articles in all the languages.
