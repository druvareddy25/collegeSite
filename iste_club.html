Indian Society for Technical Education – 

ISTE which stands for INDIAN SOCIETY OF TECHNICAL EDUCATION first started in 1941 as the Association of Principals of Technical Institutions (APTI), it was converted into "Indian Society for Technical Education" in 1968 with a view to enlarge its activities to advance the cause of technological education. The Ministry of Human Resource Development, CTE / DST /MIT / State Govts. are well associated with the ISTE for programs relating to technical education. Coming to ISTE-SNIST, every student of SREENIDHI is a part of ISTE-SNIST. It provides a good platform for the students to improve technical knowledge in various fields. 

The objectives of ISTE in SNIST are: 

• This is a place where the technical skills of a person are 

   qualified along with Organizing skills, entrepreneurship 

   skills, management skills. 

• This is a place, wherein we can develop ourselves in all 

   the aspects, required to cope up with the competition in 

   the global market. 

•  We are nurtured here to develop ourselves as good entrepreneurs.
