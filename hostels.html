Hostel

A good institution always strives to transform the students into a knowledgeable and worthy citizen. To accomplish this, good learning environment is very essential. A hostel is truly a home away from home. Students of different types hailing from different regions stay together, representing various traditions, cultures, talents, etc. The hostel gives vast scope for respecting differences of opinions, builds up a healthy union of minds and unites the hearts. The hostel life offers plenty of opportunities for growing, individually and collectively. During the stay in the hostel, the student learns to grow more loveable and capable. Besides learning good qualities and habits from others, the student realizes that he/she can also serve as a role model to others. Some of the traits in a cultured person are: remaining simple, humble, honest, open and truthful. Hostel life provides plenty of occasions to grow in all these traits. The hostel environment itself teaches the student to be generous and magnanimous and to excel in such virtues.



Hence the need of good hostel accommodation near the campus is essential. Besides, the staying place too, is required to have academic environment for study for longer duration and to prevent the students from day to day disturbances and hour to hour distraction. Hence, Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology provides high standards of accommodation and top hostel facilities for students (both boys and girls); ‘A Home Away From Home’ - designed for the modern day stay for anyone wishing to stay in peaceful and calm surroundings. The hostel is located just minutes away from the train and bus stations and a short travel from the college at Ghatkesar, about 4 km from the college campus. A unique touch is given to this hostel, as each of the spacious balconied room overlooks the open grassy meadow with billowing greenery. The cool and fresh air breeze at dawn and dusk makes one feel light and rejuvenated to work/study better. Combine this with the fact that they have just 12 rooms in each floor, the hosteller never feels overcrowded or congested. Hostel accommodation is provided in a 6 storey building about ½ km behind the Ghatkesar Bus stand. 66 four bedded rooms and 8 two bedded rooms are available for accommodation. out of which 30 four bedded rooms and 3 two bedded rooms are exclusively for girls. A total of 126 girls and 164 boys can be accommodated. Separate accommodation is provided for girls and boys. Each four bedded room has 8 electric plug points for phone charging, using of laptops, computers, etc. Hostel is administered by chief warden who is assisted by one Boys warden and one Girls warden. Both these waredens reside in the hostel premises.
